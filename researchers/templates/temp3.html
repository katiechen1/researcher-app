Why have you been nominated and what is the Women in Microfluidics & BioMEMS List?
A colleague took the time to nominate you as they consider you an important contributor to our field. The Women in Microfluidics & BioMEMS List is a community-created resource created to enhance the quality of scientific programs & representation of women in our field. The Women in Microfluidics & BioMEMS List provides a summary of research leaders – is designed to assist conference organizers with creating excellent conference offerings by highlighting women who could be included in scientific programs (e.g., plenary/keynote speakers, panelists, organizing committee members, session chairs, poster judges) and leadership positions. The Women in Microfluidics & BioMEMS List is inspired by the positive influence of anneslist.net, created by colleagues in neuroscience.
